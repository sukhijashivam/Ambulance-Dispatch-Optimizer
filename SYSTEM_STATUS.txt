📊 REAL EXAMPLE WORKFLOW:

Input:
- Emergency 1 @ Node 9 (Critical)
- Emergency 2 @ Node 5 (High)
- Emergency 3 @ Node 2 (Normal)

Processing:
1. Sort by priority (1→2→3)
2. For Emergency 1:
   • Check Ambulances 1,2,3
   • Find distances using Dijkstra
   • Select Ambulance 3 (closest: 3.5km)

✓ Ambulance 2 → Emergency 2 | Route: 3→6→8→7→5 | ETA: 10.5 min
✓ Ambulance 1 → Emergency 3 | Route: 0→2 | ETA: 6 min
